Dobrin Vasile

I am a highly motivated Electronics and Telecommunications student, entering my 3rd year of studies at the Polytechnic University of Bucharest, with a strong foundation in embedded systems, digital design, and hardware-software integration.

My academic path and extracurricular activities reflect a clear interest in the intersection between hardware and Artificial Intelligence, with hands-on experience ranging from microcontroller programming (ESP32, 8051, ARM Cortex) to FPGA development, SystemVerilog verification, and computer vision applications.

Through my participation in programs such as Google Romania AI & Tech Summer School, Infineon Summer School, Marvell ASIC Design Summer School, and Vodafone Innovation Hub, I have gained exposure to both industry practices and cutting-edge technologies. I have also demonstrated adaptability and teamwork by actively engaging in hackathons and technical clubs, where I developed innovative solutions involving IoT, robotics, and digital systems.

Currently, I am expanding my expertise in ARM Cortex architectures, Linux embedded systems, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision, aiming to pursue a career where I can contribute to the development of intelligent and efficient embedded solutions.
