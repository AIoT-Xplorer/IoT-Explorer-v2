A highly-sensitive pulse oximeter and heart-rate sensor. It measures blood oxygen saturation (SpO₂) and pulse rate.

Updates & News
August 20, 2025
A function in working for the moment: a camera flux live

For the moment, we are working on a live camera flux that will be integrated into the dashboard. This will allow users to monitor real-time humans that are in need of help or to be resuscitate.

August 19, 2025
New Sensor Integration: Pulse Oximeter Live!

We are excited to announce the successful integration of our new pulse oximeter sensor. You can now monitor real-time oxygen saturation (SpO₂) and pulse rate data directly on the dashboard. This enhancement provides a more comprehensive view of vital signs, improving our health monitoring capabilities.
