IoT-Xplorers Team β
We are the AIoT-Xplorer - Team-Beta, a recently established subgroup of students within the IoT-Xplorer initiative from the National University of Science and Technology POLITEHNICA Bucharest. Our team brings together motivated and perseverant students, united by a common interest in innovation and collaboration, with the ambition to transform ideas into meaningful results. Although new, our team builds on a strong foundation of curiosity, adaptability, and teamwork. We are committed to learning through practice, contributing to the development of the IoT-Xplorer community, and pursuing impactful projects that connect research, education, and real-world applications.
